MEET OUR TEAM

Shaun Stafford

Shaun Stafford, armed with a Masters degree in strength and conditioning from Oxford University, is regarded as one of the best personal trainers in the world. He started his career as a semi-professional rugby player before transitioning to bodybuilding and becoming a 2X WBFF World Fitness Champion.

Tracy Anderson

Tracy Anderson is a well-known name in the fitness industry. She gained recognition for her unique fitness approach "The Tracy Anderson Method", which includes Pilates inspired workouts, high repetition, and targeted muscle training. She also invented the exercise machine popularly known as the hybrid body reformer.

Sarah Lindsay

Sarah Lindsay is a three-time Olympian and 10-time British speed skating champion specialising in total body transformations. Sarah works alongside her clients in her gym using a combination of fun and dedicated training regimes. Besides this, she focuses on muscle building and fat loss using nutrition and resistance training.

Nick Mitchell

Nick Mitchell is not just a personal trainer; he is a best-selling author and the founder of Ultimate Performance (UP). Although Nick Mitchell didnt start his career as a personal trainer, his over a decade of success in the industry puts him ahead of the rest.

Matt Roberts

With over 25 years in the industry, Matt Roberts has established himself as a veteran in the fitness industry. Every client in this facility goes through physical fitness testing, 3D body shape and composition analysis, Styku laser scan, detailed blood profiling, hormone exploration, and complete dietary evaluation to ensure they set adequate goals and clear timelines.

Alexia Clark

Alexia Clark is a certified personal trainer and fitness nutrition specialist with nearly 10 years of experience training clients to reach their fitness goals. Her program provides clients with new workouts daily to keep them motivated to reach their goals. Alongside workouts, Alexia also provides nutrition support and direct accountability to clients.

Tyrone Brennand

Tyrone Brennand is a popular personal trainer, author, and founder of "Be The Fittest", also known as the leading fitness resource all through London. With nearly two decades in the industry, he has gained extensive experience in the health and fitness space to help thousands of people. Plus, he specialises in strength toning, fat loss, yoga, and toning.

Louise Parker

With over 20 years in the fitness industry, Louise Parker has been transforming the lives of her clients. Louise uses nurturing to help her clients instead of punishment because she understands the struggles associated with weight loss. Besides, Louise has helped over 20,000 clients across 50 countries transform their lives.

Brad Schoenfeld

Brad Schoenfeld is a renowned name in the fitness industry. As a professor of exercise science at Lehman College and author, he made a name for himself with his research in human performance, muscle hypertrophy, and exercise physiology. Besides, he has published over 300 peer-reviewed research articles on sports nutrition and exercise.

OUR CLASSES

BODY BLITZ

Achieve your fitness goals with high-intensity interval training (HIIT) that combines cardio and strength exercises. Burn calories, build muscle, and get fitter in each session.

Focus:

Fat loss, strength building, and overall fitness.

METABOLIC BURN

A dynamic workout designed to maximize calorie burn and boost your metabolism. This class incorporates a mix of cardio, strength training, and functional exercises.

Focus:

Metabolism boost, fat loss, and toning.

POWER SCULPT

Sculpt and tone your body with a powerful workout that combines resistance training and high-intensity cardio. Perfect for those looking to burn fat and build lean muscle.

Focus:

Muscle toning, fat loss, and strength building.

TURBO BURN

An explosive workout that blends cardio and strength exercises to ignite your metabolism and torch calories. Get ready for a high-energy class that delivers results.

Focus:

Fat burning, endurance, and muscle conditioning.

SCULPT & SHRED

A total-body workout that combines strength training and intense cardio intervals. Designed to help you shed fat, build strength, and improve overall fitness.

Focus:

Fat loss, muscle building, and cardiovascular health.

EXTREME BURN

Push your limits with this high-intensity workout that focuses on burning fat and increasing strength. Features a combination of challenging exercises and circuits.

Focus:

Fat burning, endurance, and strength.

LEAN AND MEAN

A high-energy class that combines cardio, strength, and core exercises to help you achieve a leaner physique. Designed to burn fat and improve fitness.

Focus:

Fat loss, core strengthening, and overall conditioning.
